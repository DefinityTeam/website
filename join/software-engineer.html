What we want

We're currently looking for JavaScript and TypeScript developers, experienced in web and/or server development.
In more detail, we're looking for people who are experienced using ExpressJS and WebSockets, but general node.js knowledge will be OK.

We're also looking for those who are experienced using HTML and CSS, so we can drastically improve our design.
Bootstrap is generally a no (blame the worse CEO).

In the future, we will be looking for app developers.
Either in React Native or plain Swift/Kotlin etc. Cordova is an absolute no.


What you'll be doing

- Working in a team to create things that help the world
- Developing and designing in the best way possible where everyone agrees, on-the-fly
- Creating things that can realistically compete with companies where they focus purely on the profit
- Working with other teams (the business team etc.) to create the best solutions for everyone
- Utilising and improving our sole payment processing server to keep everything easy to pay for on the user end
- Whether directly or indirectly, encouraging environmental change in the real world

What to expect

You will get to decide what you work on.
In general, provided you know what to do, you can work on any project at any point.

You work your own hours.
We will not force you to work more than you want to.
Provided you are working sufficiently to our standards, we will not consult you with any "additional work time" concerns.

Freedom of opinion.
We believe we should provide you with freedom.
You are allowed to have your own opinions and if you disagree with something internally, you can speak out.

Rewards.
Where financially reasonable, we will try to give you things like free credit on services or free early access to things you can't access.
You can see it as our thank you for working for us.


How do I apply?

Contact us directly at employment[at]definityteam[.]com with your CV and somewhere to contact you via call.
Discord tag etc. - this is so we can interview you.
Please also provide us some example projects where possible if not already provided within the CV.
