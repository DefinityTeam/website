Information
What we want

We're looking for artists who can help us create logos and branding.
You'll be helping us create the recognisable branding for our products.

What you'll be doing

- Working with the Marketing Team and the Administrative team to create branding for Definity
- Working with the software engineers to create art for our products
- Whether directly or indirectly, encouraging environmental change in the real world

What to expect

Work when you want.
If you're not motivated to make art - don't!
Work when you want to and are available.

Freedom of opinion.
We believe we should provide you with freedom.
You are allowed to have your own opinions and if you disagree with something internally, you can speak out.

Rewards.
Where financially reasonable, we will try to give you things like free credit on services or free early access to things you can't access.
You can see it as our thank you for working for us.


How do I apply?

Contact us directly at employment[at]definityteam[.]com with your CV and somewhere to contact you via call.
Discord tag etc. - this is so we can interview you.
Please also provide us your portfolio if not already provided within the CV.
