What we want

We're looking for people for people who can help us decide who to interview.
You'll be deciding who gets to be interviewed and who doesn't.

What you'll be doing

- Reading the employment email

- Working with HR and the COO to make sure people get their interview
- Updating the website when a position becomes or is no longer available
- Ensuring everyone who deserves a chance gets a chance
- Whether directly or indirectly, encouraging environmental change in the real world

What to expect

You work when you want.
Only review employment emails when you want to.
As long as you are working sufficiently, we believe you should work when you want.

Freedom of opinion.

Rewards.
Where financially reasonable, we will try to give you things like free credit on services (or even free servers!) or free early access to things you can't access.
